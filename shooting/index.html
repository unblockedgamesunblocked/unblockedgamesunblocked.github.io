shooting games

A type of action video games called shooters places a heavy emphasis on utilizing the player's available weapons to destroy the adversaries of the character.
These weapons, which are often rifles or other long-range weaponry, may be combined with grenades for covert offensive, armor for added protection, or modifications like telescopic sights to alter their behavior.
Ammunition, armor, health, and weapon upgrades for the player character are frequent resources in shooter games.
