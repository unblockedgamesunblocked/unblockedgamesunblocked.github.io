idle games

The main characteristic of an idle game's strategy—leaving the game running on its own with little to no player interaction—is what gives the genre of games its name.
Idle games are sometimes referred to as incremental games. Even though it's often necessary for advancement, interaction with the game is optional for lengthy stretches of gameplay.
